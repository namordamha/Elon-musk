Biography

Elon Musk was born in 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. He moved to the United States and became one of the most influential figures in technology.

Achievements

  • Founded PayPal, later acquired by eBay.
  • Launched the first private rocket to reach orbit.
  • Revolutionized the electric car industry with Tesla.
  • Developed reusable rocket technology.
  • Founded OpenAI to advance artificial intelligence research.
  • Proposed Hyperloop for high-speed transportation.
  • Developed AI Chatbot (Grok-3)
