Elon Musk - Biography

Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. From a young age, he showed a deep interest in computers and technology, teaching himself programming at the age of 10.

Education

He attended Pretoria Boys High School and later moved to the United States to study at the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in Physics and Economics.

Career & Achievements

  • 1995: Co-founded Zip2, a software company.
  • 1999: Created X.com, which later became PayPal.
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX to revolutionize space travel.
  • 2004: Joined Tesla Motors, helping lead the electric vehicle revolution.
  • 2015: Co-founded OpenAI to advance artificial intelligence.
  • 2016: Founded Neuralink, working on brain-computer interfaces.
  • 2023: Acquired and rebranded Twitter as X.
  • 2025: Developed AI Chatbot known as Grok-3

Vision & Future Plans

Elon Musk continues to push boundaries with ambitious projects like Mars colonization, AI development, and sustainable energy solutions.
